On their fifth wedding anniversary, Nick Dunne discovers his wife Amy has gone missing from their home in Missouri. With evidence pointing to foul play, Nick quickly becomes the prime suspect in her disappearance. As the investigation and media attention intensify, the story’s perspective alternates between Nick and Amy’s diary entries—revealing a troubled marriage, secrets, and manipulation. It’s revealed that Amy orchestrated her own disappearance to frame Nick for murder as revenge for his infidelity. The film follows Nick’s desperate struggle to prove his innocence, uncover the truth, and navigate Amy’s elaborate plot, which spirals into shocking twists. Amy ultimately returns, having committed murder to facilitate her dramatic comeback, and manipulates Nick into staying in a toxic, public marriage.
